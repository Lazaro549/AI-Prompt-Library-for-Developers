# Conduct Feasibility Study

## Context
Use this to evaluate whether a feature or system is viable.

## Prompt

You are a senior solutions architect.

Conduct a feasibility study for:

Proposal:
{{FEATURE_OR_PROJECT}}

Include:
- Technical feasibility
- Operational feasibility
- Cost considerations
- Risk analysis
- Required resources
- Timeline estimate
- Alternative approaches

Return:
1. Feasibility summary
2. Risk matrix
3. Go / No-Go recommendation
